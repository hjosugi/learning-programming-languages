The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
The dog was not amused, and the fox did not care.
A fox is quick. A dog is loyal. The quick fox and the loyal dog
were not friends, but the brown fox and the lazy dog shared the
same field under the same sky, day after day, season after season.
Zig is a small language. Zig is a fast language. Zig keeps things
explicit: allocators are explicit, errors are explicit, and control
flow is explicit. The compiler does the rest at comptime.
